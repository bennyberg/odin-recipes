Classic Tiramisu

Description

This no-bake Italian dessert layers espresso-soaked ladyfingers with a rich mascarpone cream and a dusting of cocoa powder. Elegant, creamy, and surprisingly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 250 g (9 oz) mascarpone cheese
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 100 g (½ cup) sugar
  • 200 ml (¾ cup) heavy cream
  • 200 ml (¾ cup) strong espresso, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 200 g (7 oz) ladyfinger biscuits
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale and creamy. Add mascarpone and mix until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until soft peaks form, then fold gently into mascarpone mixture.
  3. In another bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Carefully fold into the cream mixture to create a light filling.
  4. Combine espresso and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (do not soak).
  5. Arrange half the ladyfingers in a single layer in a dish. Spread half the cream mixture on top. Repeat with remaining ladyfingers and cream.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) to set.
  7. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.
