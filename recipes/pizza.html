Homemade Margherita Pizza

Description

This classic Margherita Pizza is simple, fresh, and delicious. It uses a thin crust, tangy tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and fresh basil for the perfect homemade pizza night.

Ingredients

  • 250 g (2 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet (7 g) instant yeast
  • 160 ml (⅔ cup) warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200 g (7 oz) mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 150 g (5 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Add warm water and olive oil. Stir until dough forms, then knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F). Roll out dough into a thin round base on a floured surface.
  3. Place dough on a baking tray or pizza stone. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top with mozzarella slices and sprinkle oregano. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves. Slice and serve hot.
